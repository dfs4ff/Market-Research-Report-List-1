<p><ul><li>Vertical Probe Cards</li><li>Cantilever Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS probe cards are essential tools for testing semiconductor devices, offering precision and efficiency. The vertical probe cards utilize a vertical alignment strategy, allowing for high-density interconnections and improved signal integrity, making them ideal for advanced packaging. In contrast, cantilever probe cards feature a flexible design with probes mounted on a lever mechanism, enabling effective contact with wafer surfaces and accommodating various die sizes. Both types cater to different testing needs within the semiconductor manufacturing process, enhancing overall productivity.</p></p>
