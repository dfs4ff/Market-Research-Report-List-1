<p><p>Inductive linear displacement sensors are essential in measuring position and movement across various industries. Variable reluctance sensors utilize changes in magnetic reluctance to detect displacement. Differential transformer sensors, also known as LVDTs, use electromagnetic induction to measure linear displacement with high accuracy. Eddy current sensors detect displacement by measuring changes in the electromagnetic field caused by conductive materials. Each type offers unique advantages, such as precision, durability, and suitability for different environments, appealing to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
