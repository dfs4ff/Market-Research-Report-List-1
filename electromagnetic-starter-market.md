<p><p>The electromagnetic starter market is divided into two primary types: single-phase and three-phase electromagnetic starters. Single-phase starters are designed for smaller applications and typically control single-phase motors, providing protection and efficient operation in residential or light commercial settings. In contrast, three-phase electromagnetic starters manage larger, three-phase motors commonly used in industrial environments. These starters offer enhanced performance, durability, and overload protection, making them essential for applications that require higher efficiency and reliability in power management.</p></p>
