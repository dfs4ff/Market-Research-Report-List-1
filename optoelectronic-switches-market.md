The Built-In Amplifier Type integrates the amplifier within the switch, providing a compact solution that simplifies installation and reduces components. The Built-In Power Supply type includes an integrated power supply, ensuring consistent performance and reliability while minimizing external dependencies, making it ideal for various electronic applications and systems.</p></p>
